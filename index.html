About the Doctor
A Legacy of Dermatological Excellence

Dr. Shaikh Mohd Kayum is one of Lucknow's most experienced and respected dermatologists, having practiced specialist skin and hair medicine for 37 uninterrupted years. His private clinic in Lucknow has been a trusted destination for patients seeking expert dermatological care.

Holding both MBBS and MD (Dermatology) degrees, Dr. Kayum brings exceptional diagnostic depth across medical dermatology, complex inflammatory skin disorders, hair pathology, and cosmetic dermatology. His decades of clinical experience translate into accurate, effective, and personalised treatment for every patient.
